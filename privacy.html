Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

At Brunel Peaks Cafe + Bar, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our contact form.

Information We Collect

When you submit a message through our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Name — So we know who to reply to
  • Email address — To send you a response
  • Enquiry type — To route your request appropriately (Reservation, General, Event, or Supplier enquiry)
  • Message/notes — Details about your enquiry

We do not collect: Payment information, location data, browsing history, or any other sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or reservation request
  • Send you a confirmation email
  • Improve our service based on feedback

We will not share your information with third parties, sell it, or use it for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in to our mailing list (if we launch one in the future).

How We Store Your Information

Your form submissions are processed and stored by Formspree (our form service provider). Formspree stores submissions securely and encrypted. We also receive copies of your message via email to hello@brunelpeakscafebar.com.

For more details on how Formspree handles data, see their Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We keep your enquiry and contact details until we've responded to your request (typically within 48 hours). After that, we may keep messages for a reasonable period to maintain business records, but we'll delete them if you request it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (right to be forgotten)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@brunelpeakscafebar.com with your request.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We don't collect analytics data. Your visit is completely private.

External Links

Our website may link to external websites (like Google Maps or our social media). We're not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the website means you accept any changes.

Contact Us
