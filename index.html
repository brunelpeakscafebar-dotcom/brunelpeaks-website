A Destination You Won't Forget

Nestled in the foothills of the majestic Takitimu Mountains, Brunel Peaks Cafe and Bar serves hearty fare cooked with love. We use locally-sourced gourmet ingredients whenever possible.

Our Kitchen

Every day, we create seasonally-inspired dishes using locally-sourced ingredients. Our ever-changing chalkboard menu reflects what's fresh, what's in season, and what our chef is inspired to create.

What We Serve

  • Breakfast From sunrise, freshly made meals, sandwiches and coffee
  • Lunch Hearty fare and light bites
  • Snacks & Drinks A wide selection of handmade and artisan kiwi classics
  • Dinner Seasonal offerings and specials

Our Signatures

  • Seafood Chowder Fresh fish, mussels, prawns in creamy broth
  • Venison Pie Slow-cooked venison in house-made gravy and flaky pastry
  • Battered Blue Cod Beer-battered, fried in beef tallow and served with chips, tartare sauce

Handmade Treats

  • Sweet Treats Freshly baked daily in our kitchen
  • Pies & Pastries House-made with love and local ingredients
  • Coffee & Drinks Craft beverages made with care
